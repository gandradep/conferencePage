Processing Community Day 2020

Processing Community Day will take place on Thursday, October 10, 2020, for which we will have national and international guests who work in the field of Art, Design and New Media.

During this day, the guests will give talks and master workshops in which they will share their projects and experience with the use of free software and the application of these tools in various fields.
